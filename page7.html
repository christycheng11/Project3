Chapter 2 (continued)

--The Betrayal--




"Your love is overwhelming."
Each lash of words sting at you, slowly breaking you down.
You question your worth, the deep suffering that leaves you more lonely
than when you were alone.
You are reduced to an empty shell, bound to the momentary attention you do receive,
the false promise of change.
You lose yourself, your happiness, as your heart is
slowly crushed by the very person you entrusted it to.
