Journey to Finding Love

More About This Project





This project speaks alot to me as I have recently gotten out of a toxic relationship.
This journal entails what I have gone through and served as some sense of closure/more self-reflection.
With a very supportive system of family and friends, I was able to see myself in a new positive light.
Now, I am working towards my goals like doing well in college
while balancing working a job related to my future career field.
Not only do I hope to tell my story through my drawings and excerpts,
but also the story of many other people who have also experienced the same.

-- Christy
