--The Rise--




How To Deal With A Painful Experience

Let it hurt.
Let it bleed.
Let it heal.
And let it go.

--Nikita Gil--

After many long walks with the gentle touch of the wind brushed against your face,
you realise that you cannot fix anyone.
Your love was not meant to be a cage, but a gift worth appreciating.
However, you dread the endless nights staying up weeping,
the terrible memories that would be try to tear you down,
or resisting the urge to run back.
