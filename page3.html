Chapter 1 (continued)

--The Fall--





After the endless late night talks held against
their warm chest, you begin to trust again.
They pop into your head mercilessly when you wake up in the morning.
Their being outshines every thought that runs through your head.
As you stare into the night sky, the stars remind you of their bright smile.
You are happy, and before you realize it yourself,
you are falling hard and prayed they felt the same way.
